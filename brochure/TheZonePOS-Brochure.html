FSR + QSR Dual Mode

Switch seamlessly between Full-Service (table-based) and Quick-Service (counter-based) modes. Manage dine-in, togo, pickup, and delivery from a single screen. Perfect for restaurants that need both sit-down and fast-casual service.

Visual Table Map

Smart Payment
